The pre-1:1 briefing, five new ways

Darren found the current final page confusing — it shows seven equally-weighted boxes and asks the manager to pick one of eleven layouts. Each direction below keeps the exact same seven pieces of content, but organises them so the manager knows where to look first and what to actually do. Same real brief in all five.

Manager prepping a 1:1 with Priya Medium confidence
01  Focus

One Thing First

Radical hierarchy. The question to open with is the whole hero; everything else is quiet support underneath. The manager sees the single most important move the instant the page loads — no hunting.

1:1 with Priya

Likely theme: she may be weighing an internal move.

Open with

"What part of the new project has your attention right now?"

How sure is this: medium
Listen for

  • Energy when she talks about mentoring
  • Hesitation around the roadmap
Don't assume
  • That the internal move is about money
  • Promising a title change
  • Comparing her to Sam
During the 1:1

Let her finish before offering solutions.

Knowing what would make her stay for the next year.
