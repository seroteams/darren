● SERO IMPROVEMENTS — WHAT GOT BETTER
  • 1The 1:1 screen became a coaching companion.Full-screen split: the question on the left, a private coaching panel on the right — the manager is guided while they talk, not left alone with a text box.
  • 2Scores you can trust — never a bare number.Each of the four reads (Wellbeing, Engagement, Clarity, Growth) moves live and shows the reason in plain words, taken straight from the engine's own read of the answer.
  • 3Room for coaching in the moment.A Support / Live-scores toggle, with space for "how to ask" and "what to listen for" on each question. (The bit that auto-writes those hints is parked — see below.)
  • 4A check-in can't quietly become an appraisal.A guard now watches the score reasoning: if performance-review language slips into a wellbeing check-in, it's flagged — and the engine's words are never secretly reworded.
  • 5All manager-only.Every hint and every reason is for the manager's eyes — the person being managed never sees any of it.
