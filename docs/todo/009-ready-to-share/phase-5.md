# Phase 5 — Feedback + one-pager

**Part of:** [PLAN.md](PLAN.md) · **Track:** A (ship-blocker) · **Status:** ⬜

## Goal
Alpha testers can tell us what broke in one click, and anyone new can read a single page that explains
what Sero is and what to expect.

## Changes
- A simple in-app feedback route — a link/button that lets a tester send a short note (and reaches Carl).
- A one-pager: what Sero is, what to do first, what to expect, and that it's an early alpha.

## Not in this phase
- Analytics dashboards or fancy feedback tooling — one plain channel is enough.
- Marketing site / pricing.

## Done when
- [ ] A tester can send feedback from inside the app and it reaches Carl.
- [ ] The one-pager exists and explains Sero in plain language.
- [ ] Product owner has tested the scenarios below and said go.

## Test scenarios — for the product owner
Walk through these yourself. Next phase waits for your green light.
1. **Send feedback** — From inside the app, send a test note. You (Carl) should receive it. ❌ Not OK if it goes nowhere or is hard to find.
2. **The one-pager** — Open the one-pager as if you'd never seen Sero. In a minute you should know what it is and what to do first. ❌ Not OK if it's jargon-y or missing the "early alpha" framing.
